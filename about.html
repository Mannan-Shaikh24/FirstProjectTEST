Who We Are

Shaikh Interiors is a premier interior design firm dedicated to transforming spaces into functional works of art. Founded by award-winning designer Mikasa Ackerman, our team brings together 50+ years of combined experience in residential and commercial design.

We believe that great design should be accessible to everyone. Our approach combines aesthetic excellence with practical solutions to create spaces that reflect your personality while serving your everyday needs.

Founder & Lead Designer
